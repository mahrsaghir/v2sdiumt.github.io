Task List in Order:

Last date of submission: 11 May, 2022

Create a Critical Analysis on “Why Lahore Lost its status as city of gardens?”

Collect data on plants that grow in Lahore and tell the benefit of each plant in 2-3 lines. Attach picture of each plant. What are Miyawaki Forests, the benefits of Miyawaki forests and suggest ways as to how Lahore’s administration can establish more Miyawaki forests.

Collect data on resident and migratory species of birds in Lahore and explain the reason as to why the population has declined in Lahore. Suggest ways to improve the avian biodiversity in Lahore.

Collect data on CSR Organizations, CSR activities of Companies, Restaurants and NGOs providing free food and free-to-eat meal spots.

Collect Data on online platforms providing community services in Pakistan.

Plant 20 Trees in your locality outside your house. Take a picture and video of each plantation activity.

Make 1 suspended bird feeder and take picture and video of it.
